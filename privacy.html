Privacy Policy and Data Deletion for CashLens

Effective Date: July 19, 2026

Privacy Policy

This privacy policy is applicable to the CashLens app (hereinafter referred to as "Application") for mobile devices, which was developed by Monitrix (hereinafter referred to as "Service Provider") as a Free service. This service is provided "AS IS".

1. What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?
User Provided Information:
The Application acquires the information you supply when you register. Authentication is handled via Google OAuth (Firebase Auth). We collect:
Your name and profile picture that you input via profile page(for personalization).
Your email address.
Financial transaction data you manually enter or that is processed from SMS.
SMS Data (Sensitive opt-in Permission):
CashLens uses the READ_SMS permission to automatically detect and add financial transactions if opted in.
Privacy Guarantee: All SMS processing happens locally on your device.
No Data Transfer: Your raw SMS messages are never uploaded to our servers, shared with third parties, or transmitted outside your device.

2. Third-Party Services
The Application utilizes third-party services that have their own Privacy Policies. These services aid in authentication, analytics, and subscription management:
Google Play Services: google.com
Google Analytics for Firebase: firebase.google.com
Firebase Crashlytics: firebase.google.com

3. Data Sync and Storage
Local Storage: We use Hive for fast, local data access on your device.

4. Data Deletion and Retention Policy
The Service Provider will retain your data for as long as you use the Application.
In-App Deletion: You can use the "Clear All Data" button within the App settings to instantly delete all data on device.
Account Deletion Request: Users can request full account deletion by emailing us at simply.chess.2.009@gmail.com. We will remove all associated data from our Firebase servers within 14 days of the request.

6. Children's Privacy
The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 years of age. If we discover a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will delete it immediately.

7. Security
We provide electronic and procedural safeguards to protect the information we process. While we endeavor to provide reasonable security, please be aware that no security system can prevent all potential breaches.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating this page. Continued use of the Application is deemed approval of all changes.
